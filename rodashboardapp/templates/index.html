Romania in Numbers

Get to Know Romania in 4 Sentences and 8 Plots.

A selected list of facts about my home country using data from the world bank.
This simple dashboard uses 8 descriptive plots that give you a general idea of how Romania is doing economically, socially and from a sustainability standpoint.
Spoiler: not great, not terrible, but definetly worth a visiting (once the COVID-19 pandemic is over)!

Our country's economy is not the strongest...

Romania in 2018 had the lowest GDP per capita in the list.

...and stuff gets more expensive fast.

Since 2010, our CPI is the highest and highest growing in the list.

We're the only country in the list with a declining population...

Romania is the only country in the list with a declining population for 10 years up until 2018 (latest date).

...but we're making more and more babies.

Fastest growing fertility rate since 2013 and second highest after France.

We're emitting very little CO2 emissions...

Country in the list with lowest CO2 emissions per capita and declining.

... and doing our best to produce renewable electricity.

In 2016 our renewable electricity share was second only to Austria (in this list).

Finally, more than half of us like living in cities...

Around 55% of population lived in cities in 2018.

...and everybody here loves the internet (my grandma is my biggest Facebook fan)!

Fastest growing internet users: 30% increase in 10 years (reaching 70% of total population in 2018), versus Poland, second highest growth, with only 15% growth (reaching 75%).
